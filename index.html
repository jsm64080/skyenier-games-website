ABOUT US

There is no point in making games for anything other than passion. That's the fuel that drives us to make the best, most passion filled games we can make.

After all, do you know how hard it is to make games?? It's actually ridiculous. I wish I picked an easier passion, TRUST ME!

Starting off as an aspiring game music composer in late 2022, I started my journey knowing absolutely nothing and having zero aspiration to learn to code. I'm not doing all of that! Coding is impossible!

Fast forward two years later, having essentially burnt out and given up on music and started a job that had me working 56 hours a week, I discovered what I really wanted to do in games:
make them.

Learn more about my sob story.

Pseudoworker is a 2D-3D hybrid game about filling in for your identical twin brother at the
popular restaurant L'Anomalie (against his will).

The problem is? Henry has zero restaurant experience, and despite looking the same, he is the
complete opposite of his brother! He's a act-before-you-think troublemaker who is absolutely
having none of it if it isn't fun, while Roscoe is the model worker, a responsible, kind, and
caring individual.

Will you successfully make it through your 8 hour shift without anyone finding out you are Roscoe,
or will you mess around a bit and see what happens?
